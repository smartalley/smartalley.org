Our Mission

Smart Alley devotes itself to fostering a community space through the revitalization of neglected alley spaces. We travel house to house, meeting with neighborhood communities and pitching innovative ideas on how to restore the alleys. Shared goals, connection, and planning are often all that neighbors need to empower themselves and work together to build both stronger neighborhood and a stronger community.

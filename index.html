Our Mission

Smart Alley devotes itself to fostering a community space focused on revitalization of neglected alley spaces. What was once used as a vibrant social space, the alleys behind certain neighborhood homes have become service oriented, bare and abandoned grounds. We travel house to house, meeting with neighborhood communities and pitching our innovative ideas. Simple solutions such as murals, community gardens, and tiny libraries can transform alleys into welcoming gathering spaces.

Collaborate and Organize

By creating a message board, we can connect all of the members in a neighborhood who wish to be a part of it. With this, we can post about upcoming events, collaborate with you, and provide ideas for a better common space. Certain ideas include: implementing free tiny libraries, community bench gardens, improved lighting, murals, and setting up surveillance cameras.
In the message board, we can also organize neighborhood projects such as cleaning parties, barbeques, and art projects, all with the final goal of bringing together the people of the community through a shared common space.
